About InAmigos Foundation

Non-profit working across education, environment, animal welfare and youth development.

Who we are

Established on 23 September 2020 by Govind Shukla, InAmigos Foundation is a registered Section 8 NGO with CSR-1 compliance, NITI Aayog registration and ISO 9001:2015 certification. We work to improve quality of life through education, skill-building, community welfare and sustainable environmental initiatives.

Mission

Empower underprivileged communities through education, skills training, women-led initiatives, and environmental stewardship to enable sustainable livelihoods.

Vision

A socially inclusive India where children access quality learning, women lead economic change, animals are treated with compassion, and nature is protected for future generations.

Values
  • Compassion & Respect
  • Transparency & Accountability
  • Community-led Solutions
  • Innovation in low-cost, high-impact programs

How we create impact

We combine direct-action programs (food & clothing distribution, school support, animal rescue), awareness campaigns (#InAmigos), skill-building internships and partnerships with corporates and campus chapters. Programs measure outcomes — learners supported, livelihoods improved, saplings planted — to iteratively improve impact.

Volunteer & Internship Opportunities

Grow with InAmigos — learn, lead, and contribute

Why join InAmigos?

  • Hands-on experience delivering social programs across domains
  • Mentorship from experienced practitioners and community leaders
  • Certificate & recommendation letters for meritorious interns
  • Opportunity to scale pilot projects and test innovative ideas

Internship structure

Our Project VIKAS internships include fieldwork, research, program delivery and outcome measurement. Interns work on scoped deliverables and receive mentorship and placement support.

Volunteer benefits

  • Skill-building: facilitation, community outreach, basic data collection
  • Networking with allied NGOs, local leaders and corporates
  • Travel support for regional activities where applicable

How to apply

Send your CV and a short note about interest areas. Selected interns are assigned mentors and project leads.
